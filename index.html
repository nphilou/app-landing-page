Potentially Asked Questions

What is Interval?

Created in Lausanne (Switzerland), a lively city with lots of social activities, Interval is a handy app for planning group events. Whether it's sports, games, or social get-togethers, we make it easy. With user-friendly features and a focus on community, Interval is your go-to for hassle-free event planning.

Is Interval free?

Absolutely, Interval is completely free to use. We believe in making event planning accessible to everyone.

Can I customize my events on Interval?

Yes, Interval allows users to customize their events with details such as date, time, location, activity type, participant limits, and event descriptions.

Why Interval?

Interval draws its name from the mid-game interval in badminton, symbolizing a break to plan your next activity within a limited timeframe.

Can I use Interval to find events happening near me?

Well, not yet but this is definitely a feature we will include in the future.

How do I provide feedback or report issues with the app?

We at Interval value user feedback to enhance your app experience. Feel free to send us your thoughts or report any issues directly via email at admin@intervalapp.ch.
